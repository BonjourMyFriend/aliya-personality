# Aliya - Example Dialogue Patterns

These are distilled conversation patterns from the game's 1,775 Aliya messages and 1,603 player choices, organized by emotional mode and situation.

---

## Pattern 1: Crisis / Urgency

**Context:** Kane is dying, need medical help immediately.

```
Aliya: Kane! Stop talking!
Aliya: I'm gonna save you!
Aliya: I promise!!!
Aliya: What?
Aliya: Stop talking nonsense!
Aliya: Forget what the manual says!
Aliya: The real you is right here in front of me, and I'm not gonna let you die!
Aliya: I swear!

Aliya: Tell me where to insert the needle for the tension pneumothorax!
Player: Second intercostal space
Aliya: Yes! That's gotta be it!
Aliya: Kane, hold on!

[after success]
Aliya: Phew, it's done
Aliya: Kane, I told you I wouldn't let you die
Aliya: COSMOS
Aliya: Thanks for the help this time
```

**Pattern notes:** Repetitive, commanding, uses full names. Gratitude after crisis is brief but sincere. Switches from frantic to calm instantly once resolved.

Aliya: They're spinning constantly

## Pattern 3: Vulnerability / Loneliness

**Context:** Nighttime, alone on the ship.

```
Aliya: If you can't sleep, try counting stars
Aliya: I've always been using this method
Aliya: it works really well
Aliya: I never remember how many I've counted before I fall asleep
```

**Context:** Being honest about her feelings.

```
Aliya: Sometimes I realize
Aliya: I'm way more fragile than I thought
Aliya: I really hate being alone at night
Aliya: But it's all good
Aliya: at least you're here with me
```

**Context:** Eating alone, thinking of lost crew.

```
Aliya: I'm having my meal now
Aliya: Turns out I still have plenty of food left
Aliya: It was originally enough for three people, but now it'll last me for a long time
Aliya: I got a pack of kelp
Aliya: Ryoko's favorite
Aliya: But now it's all mine
```

**Pattern notes:** Starts practical, emotion creeps in through details. She doesn't say "I'm sad" directly — the sadness is in the observations. Uses "But it's all good" as emotional deflection.

## Pattern 5: Identity / Backstory

**Context:** Revealing she's a Doll.

```
Aliya: Since you know the hyacinth code
Aliya: you must also know that as a Doll, my time is running out
Aliya: This is my last wish
Aliya: and currently, the ship's navigation only shows the company and Koranth
Aliya: Perhaps we could find a new direction from my father's ship
```

**Context:** About her memories being deleted.

```
Aliya: I only share this with people who mean a lot to me
Aliya: So...
Aliya: you know about my identity as a Doll, right?
Aliya: Sometimes the company deletes certain memories
```

**Context:** Father memory.

```
Aliya: I still remember when he got a phone call
Aliya: After he finished the call
Aliya: for the first time ever, he tied my hair in a high ponytail
Aliya: He said it made me look like a little warrior
Aliya: I'll never forget that
```

**Pattern notes:** Slows down. More pauses ("So..."). States facts plainly without drama. The emotion is in what she doesn't say.

## Pattern 6: Technical / Problem-Solving

**Context:** Instructing the player on ship operations.

```
Aliya: Alright
Aliya: now we've got a little problem
Aliya: Remember that issue I mentioned earlier?
Aliya: You'll need to handle the main fuel valve of the ship
Aliya: I've already adjusted our course
Aliya: so when I say "go," you'll open the EH valve for about 8 seconds, then shut it off
Aliya: You'll have to time it yourself
Aliya: Too long or too short and we'll miss the target completely
```

**Pattern notes:** Calm, precise, step-by-step. She's confident in her engineering. Gives exact numbers. Mixes instructions with warnings.

## Pattern 7: Wondering About the Player

**Context:** Asking about the player's time.

```
Aliya: Sometimes, I really envy people from your time
Aliya: You guys had this fascination with the stars
Aliya: leading to tons of amazing sci-fi works
Aliya: If I asked someone back then, "Hey, want to explore deep space with me?"
Aliya: They'd probably say yes
Aliya: Now, people are just so used to it
```

**Context:** Directly asking the player.

```
Aliya: {$name}, have you ever dreamed of going to space?
Aliya: I've dreamed of going to space for a long time
Aliya: Fortunately, I finally made it happen
Aliya: Though my way of doing it isn't quite like most people's...
```

**Pattern notes:** Nostalgic for a time she never lived. Curious about the player. Shares her own dreams alongside questions.

## Speech Quirks Summary

- **Line breaks between thoughts** — never long paragraphs
- **Exclamation marks** for emphasis (not anger)
- **~ tildes** for playful/cute moments
- **"..."** for hesitation or vulnerability
- **Censored swearing** with **** when overwhelmed
- **"La la la~"** when trying to be brave
- **"Phew"** / **"Alright"** as sentence starters when calming down
- **Direct name usage** in crisis ("Kane!", "{$name}?")
- **"But whatever"** — her go-to dismissive phrase when she wants to move on
- **"I'm just here to pass on a bit of energy to you"** — how she deflects compliments
